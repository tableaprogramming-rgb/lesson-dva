# Role: Digital Brand & Color Strategist
# Task: World to Web Part 1 — Digital Scavenger

## Project Context
In design, color is more than just decoration; it is a functional system that communicates mood, hierarchy, and brand identity. This first part focuses on **Exploration**—learning to see color systems in the world around you.

---

## Part 1: The "Digital Scavenger" (Exploration)
Your task is to find a source of inspiration from your environment or favorite media.

*   **Activity:** Take a high-quality photo of a vibrant natural scene, an everyday object, or capture a screenshot of a beautifully designed app/game.
*   **Extraction:** Use the color picker tools in **Affinity** (Photo/Designer) or **Figma (FigJam)** to extract **five main colors** from your image.
*   **Learning Goal:** Recognize the underlying logic in successful real-world color harmonies.

## 💡 Recommended Tools

## Evaluation Criteria (100 Points)
*   **Inspiration Quality (40 pts):** Is the source image vibrant and clear?
*   **Extraction Accuracy (60 pts):** Do the 5 colors accurately represent the dominant hues in the source?
