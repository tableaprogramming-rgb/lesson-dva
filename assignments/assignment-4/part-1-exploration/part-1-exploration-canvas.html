Digital Brand & Color Strategist

Part 1: The "Digital Scavenger" (Exploration)

Project Context

This first part focuses on Exploration—learning to pick color systems from the world around you. You will identify vibrant inspiration and extract the colors that make it work.

Task: Find Your Inspiration

  • Capture: Take a photo of a nature scene, an everyday object, or a screenshot of your favorite app.
  • Extract: Use the color picker tools in Affinity (Photo/Designer) or Figma (FigJam) to pick five main colors from the image.
