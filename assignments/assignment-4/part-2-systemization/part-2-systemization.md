# Role: Digital Brand & Color Strategist
# Task: World to Web Part 2 — Building the Palette (Systemization)

## Project Context
A collection of hues is not a palette until you define their roles. This part of the challenge focuses on **Systemization**—turning your extracted colors into a functional design system.

---

## Part 2: Building the Palette (Systemization)
Using the colors you extracted in Part 1, you must now transform them into a **Design System** using the 60-30-10 Rule.

### Assigning Roles (The 60-30-10 Rule)
Identify which of your 5 colors will play which role:
1.  **Primary (60%):** The dominant background or neutral tone. This sets the overall mood.
2.  **Secondary (30%):** Used for headers, cards, or larger elements. It should complement the primary.
3.  **Accent (10%):** A bold, high-contrast "pop" color reserved for Call-to-Action (CTA) buttons or critical info.

### Accessibility Check
Use the contrast checking accessibility features in **Figma** (or a contrast checker plugin in **Affinity**) to ensure your Text and Accent colors are readable against your Primary background.

---

### 📌 Example: Part 2 Submission
> Using the tropical sunset palette from Part 1:
> 
> | Role | Color | Hex Code | Usage |
> |------|-------|----------|-------|
> | **Primary (60%)** | Soft Yellow | #F9C784 | Page background |
> | **Secondary (30%)** | Deep Navy | #1C2541 | Headers and cards |
> | **Accent (10%)** | Vibrant Orange | #FF8C42 | CTA buttons |
> | Text Color | Deep Navy | #1C2541 | Body text |
> | Highlight | Rose Pink | #C46B7D | Hover states |
> 
> **Accessibility Result:** Deep Navy (#1C2541) on Soft Yellow (#F9C784) passes WCAG AA with a high contrast ratio. ✅

## Output Format
Please submit:
1.  **Color Palette Specs:** A table of your 5 colors with their **Hex Codes** and assigned **Roles** (Primary, Secondary, Accent).
2.  **Accessibility Report:** A screenshot or statement showing that your background and text color combination passes a contrast check.

---

## Evaluation Criteria (100 Points)
*   **Role Logic (50 pts):** Proper application of the 60-30-10 rule. Do the colors make sense in their assigned roles?
*   **Accessibility Compliance (50 pts):** Does the text remain readable against the background?
