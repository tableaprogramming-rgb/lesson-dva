Part 2: Building the Palette (Systemization)

Project Context

Now it's time to turn your collection of hues into a functional design system. You will define specific roles for each color using professional design principles.

The 60-30-10 Rule

Assign each of your extracted colors from Part 1 a specific role:

Primary (60%) Main background or dominant neutral tone.
Secondary (30%) Headers or large sections that complement.
Accent (10%) Bold contrast for buttons and critical info.

⚠️ Accessibility Check: Use the contrast checking accessibility features in Figma (or a contrast checker plugin in Affinity) to ensure your text is readable against your background colors!

📌 Example: Part 2 Role Assignment

Role Swatch Hex Usage
Primary (60%) #F9C784 Page background
Secondary (30%) #1C2541 Headers and cards
Accent (10%) #FF8C42 CTA buttons

✅ Accessibility: Deep Navy on Soft Yellow = High contrast ratio (WCAG AA Pass)

Submission Format

  1. Role Assignment Table: Roles (Primary, Secondary, Accent), Swatches, and Hex Codes.
  2. Accessibility Report: Screenshot or statement confirming contrast ratio pass.
