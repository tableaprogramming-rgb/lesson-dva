Digital Illustrator & Line Artist

Assignment 2: Line Art Composition — Theme: "School"

Project Context

In this assignment, you will explore the power of Line as a fundamental element of design. You are tasked with creating a digital illustration using Affinity, focusing strictly on line-based representation. By limiting your tools, you will learn how to communicate form, depth, and texture through varied line weights and strokes.

The Brief

Create a digital line art illustration based on the theme "School". This can be anything related to the academic environment: a classroom scene, a desktop with supplies, or architectural details.

Technical Constraints (Crucial):
  • Software: Affinity (Designer or Photo)
  • Tool: Strictly the Paint Brush Tool only
  • Style: Pure line art (No fills, no gradients, no effects)
  • Canvas: A4 or Letter size at 300 DPI

Core Requirements

Theme Interpretation Clearly represent the "School" theme through your choice of subject.
Line Quality Use varied line weights to create depth, emphasis, and texture.
Composition Effective use of the canvas (Rule of Thirds, Leading Lines).

Submission Format

  1. Digital Art: High-res PNG or PDF.
  2. Source File: The raw .afdesign or .afphoto file.
  3. Rationale: 100-150 words explaining your theme and line work choices.

💡 Teacher's Tip: The "Single Tool" Challenge

Using only the Paint Brush tool masters your hand-eye coordination!

  • Pressure Sensitivity: If using a tablet, vary pressure for dynamic widths.
  • Stabilizer: If using a mouse, enable "Stabilizer" in Affinity for smoother lines.

Evaluation Criteria
