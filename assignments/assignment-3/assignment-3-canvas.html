Assignment 3: Geometric Composition — Line & Shape Integration

Project Context

In the previous assignment, you explored the power of Line to create form and depth. Now, you will transition into the world of Shape. By combining the precision of lines with the weight of geometric forms, you will create a cohesive composition using Affinity Designer.

The Brief

Design a balanced composition representing a theme using only geometric shapes and line work.

Theme Options (Choose One):
  • Urban Jungle: Architecture meets nature.
  • Celestial Voyage: Space, planets, and energy.
  • Mechanical Life: Biology reimagined as machines.
Technical Constraints:
  • Software: Affinity Designer
  • Tools: Shapes, Pen Tool, Stroke & Fill
  • Palette: 3-4 colors max
  • Canvas: 1080px x 1080px

Core Requirements

  • Integration: Lines and shapes must interact meaningfully.
  • Balance: Demonstrate clear Symmetrical or Asymmetrical balance.
  • Complexity: At least 5 distinct shapes and varied line weights.
  • Negative Space: Use the canvas background to define your forms.

Submission Format

  1. Digital Composition: High-res PNG or JPG.
  2. Source File: The raw .afdesign file.
  3. Rationale: 100-200 words explaining your theme and technical choices.

💡 Teacher's Tip: "Form follows Function"

Shapes provide visual "weight" and stability, while Lines provide "direction" and movement. Experiment with overlapping shapes and different stroke alignments (Inner vs. Outer) to create unique visual intersections.

Evaluation Criteria

Criteria Points
Technical Execution (Clean paths/Tools) 30
Concept & Integration (Line + Shape Fusing) 30
Composition & Balance 30
Technical Compliance (Palette/Constraints) 10
Total 100
